Your morning briefing has three cards. Here is how to read each one.

## RISK

The RISK card flags something that dropped below a threshold or behaved unexpectedly overnight.

**What you will see:**
- What happened (e.g. "Meta ROAS dropped 38% vs your 7-day baseline")
- Why it happened (e.g. "CPMs rose 22% with no change in conversion rate, indicating a media cost issue")
- What to do (e.g. "Pause the three lowest-performing ad sets and reallocate budget to the top performer")

**How to act on it:**
Read the recommendation first. If you agree with the diagnosis, follow the action. If you want more detail, click **Investigate** to open a full analysis in ClickBoss.

<Note>Not every RISK is urgent. Some are informational. The briefing tells you the severity so you can prioritise.</Note>

## GROWTH

The GROWTH card surfaces something that is performing better than expected.

**What you will see:**
- What is outperforming (e.g. "Google Ads branded campaigns returning 6.2x ROAS vs 4.1x baseline")
- Why it matters (e.g. "Efficiency has improved for three consecutive days")
- What to do (e.g. "Increase daily budget by 20% to capitalise on the current performance window")

**How to act on it:**
Growth signals decay. A campaign outperforming today may not be next week. If the recommendation makes sense, act on it the same day.

## FORECAST

The FORECAST card shows whether you are on track to hit your monthly targets.

**What you will see:**
- Current pace vs target for revenue, spend, and CPA
- Projected month-end number based on current trajectory
- The gap between your projection and your target

**How to read the gap:**

| Signal | What it means |
|---|---|
| On track | Current pace will hit target if it holds |
| At risk | You are behind and need to adjust |
| Over budget | Spend is tracking above target |
| Ahead of target | Revenue is running ahead. Consider whether targets need revising |

**How to act on it:**
If the forecast shows a gap, look at the RISK card first. Often the same issue that created a risk signal is also what is pulling the forecast off track.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if I do not have targets set?">
    The RISK and GROWTH cards still work without targets. The FORECAST card will have limited output. Set your monthly targets in Project Settings for the full forecast.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I see yesterday's briefing?">
    Yes. Open your project dashboard and scroll to the briefing history section.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why did I not receive a briefing today?">
    Check that at least one integration is connected and active. If a connected platform had an API outage overnight, the briefing may be incomplete. ClickBoss will flag this in the briefing itself.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does 'vs baseline' mean?">
    ClickBoss compares today's metrics against a rolling baseline, typically a 7-day or 30-day average depending on the metric. This filters out day-of-week variation and surfaces genuine changes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
