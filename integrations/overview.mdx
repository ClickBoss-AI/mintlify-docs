## How connections work

All integrations connect via OAuth. You are redirected to the platform, you authorise access, and you are returned to ClickBoss. No passwords are stored.

Once connected, ClickBoss reads your data on a continuous basis and includes it in your morning briefing.

## Workspace-level sharing

Integrations are stored at the workspace level. If one team member connects Google Ads, everyone in the workspace can use that connection in their projects. You do not need every team member to have admin access to every platform.
