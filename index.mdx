ClickBoss is an AI-powered growth analyst for marketing teams and ecommerce brands. It connects to your ad platforms, analytics, and revenue data, monitors everything around the clock, and tells you exactly what needs your attention each morning.

No dashboards to check. No reports to build. Just the three things you need to act on today.

## What ClickBoss does

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Delivers a morning briefing with your top priorities">
    Every day at 8:30am, ClickBoss sends you three cards: what is at risk, what is growing, and whether you are on track for your monthly targets. Each card includes the diagnosis and a recommended action. You read it over coffee and know exactly where to focus.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Runs automated playbooks on your live data">
    Playbooks are saved analysis templates. Check creative fatigue, monitor budget pacing, surface underperforming keywords, track funnel drop-offs. Run them manually or set them to run on a schedule and surface findings in your briefing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lets you investigate anything by asking a question">
    Open an investigation and ask ClickBoss a question in plain English. It pulls data from your connected platforms, runs the analysis, and gives you a structured answer with supporting data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Connects to the platforms you already use">
    Meta Ads, Google Ads, Google Analytics 4, Shopify, and HubSpot. All connections are read-only via OAuth. ClickBoss never writes to or modifies anything in your accounts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
