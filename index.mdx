ClickBoss monitors your marketing stack around the clock. Every morning at 8:30am it delivers a briefing with your top three priorities: what is at risk, what is growing, and whether you are on track for your monthly targets.

This documentation covers everything you need to connect your platforms, read your briefing, and get the most out of ClickBoss.
