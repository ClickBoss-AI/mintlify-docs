## What you need

- A ClickBoss account. [Sign up free](https://www.clickboss.ai/pricing) if you do not have one.
- Admin or advertiser access to at least one of: Meta Ads, Google Ads, Google Analytics 4, Shopify, or HubSpot.

## Step 1: Create a project

A project is a container for one brand or client. All your connected platforms, briefings, and insights live inside a project.

1. Log in to ClickBoss.
2. Click **New Project** on the home screen.
3. Give your project a name (usually the brand or business name).
4. Click **Create**.

## Step 2: Connect a platform

1. Inside your project, click **Connect a platform**.
2. Choose the platform you want to connect first.
3. Follow the OAuth flow to authorise ClickBoss to read your data.
4. Once connected, ClickBoss immediately runs a scan on your account.

<Tip>You do not need to connect everything at once. Start with your primary paid media platform and add more later.</Tip>

## Step 3: Set your targets

ClickBoss compares your daily performance against targets to generate RISK and FORECAST signals. Without targets, the briefing still works but the forecast section will be limited.

1. Go to **Project Settings**.
2. Under **Targets**, enter your monthly revenue target, ad spend target, and CPA target.
3. Save.

## Step 4: Wait for your first briefing

Your morning briefing arrives at 8:30am. If you connected your platform today, your first full briefing will arrive tomorrow morning.

<Note>ClickBoss also runs an immediate scan when you first connect a platform. You can view early insights on your project home screen before the morning briefing arrives.</Note>
